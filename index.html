Traditional Indonesian Recipes

Traditional Indonesian food is known for its rich and diverse flavors, influenced by a variety of spices, herbs, and cultural influences.

A collection of recipes from Indonesia!

  • Gado-Gado

    A popular salad featuring a mix of blanched or steamed vegetables, tofu, tempeh, and hard-boiled eggs, all drizzled with a peanut sauce dressing.

  • Rendang

    A flavorful and tender coconut beef stew, cooked with a mixture of rich spices and coconut milk until the liquid evaporates and the meat is infused with a deep, aromatic flavor.

  • Sate

    Grilled skewers of marinated and seasoned meat, usually served with a peanut sauce. Chicken and beef satay are common, and the skewers are often accompanied by cucumber and rice cakes.
