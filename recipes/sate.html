Sate

Satay is a popular Indonesian dish consisting of skewered and grilled meat, usually served with a flavorful dipping sauce.

Ingredients

  • 500 gr of tenderloin, cut into dice shape
  • 2 green paprika, cut into dice shape
  • 2 yellow paprika, cut into dice shape
  • 3 green onion, cut into dice shape
  • Satay sticks
  • 2 tablespoon of sweet soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoon of cooking oil
  • Â½ tea spoon of pepper powder

Method

  1. Pin the ingredient into the satay sticks by following these order: meat, yellow paprika, meat, green paprika, meat and green onion. Soak it into the spices of cooking oil and sweet soy sauce
  2. Bake the satay on burning charcoal until it is done
  3. Serve it on a plate and pour the mixture of spicy sauce on it
  4. satay is ready
